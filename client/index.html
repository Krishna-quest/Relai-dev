discover relai

Hire service providers and manage the entire process in one click

Relai is an intelligent real estate marketplace with integrated workflow tool which allows users to easily manage property transaction process and renovation projects. In the platform, users can find all relevant real estate professionals to buy/sell and renovate their property in one click.


Being a regulated entity, Relai provides a reliable and trustworthy environment for all parties. Thanks to our proprietary workflow tool, Relai makes the process completely transparent and enables elimination of multiple ‘grey’ zones which creates the opportunity for hidden costs and delays.

features

What’s great about our platform?

Money Saving

Relai platform is free for all users(buyer/ sellers/ renovators). Competition between service providers allows users to get cheaper fees for professional services.

Transparency

Relai workflow tool allows users to be in control of the transaction and/or renovation process. Users can check progress at any time and taking actions to avoid undue delays.

Building Trust

Relai helps build trust by bringing in transparency and reducing risks. All professionals go through due diligence process before being onboarded to the Relai platform. Users (buyers/ sellers/ renovators) go through identity check.
